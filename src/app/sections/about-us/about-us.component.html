About Us

   Skill Levels


We welcome all skill levels! Our members range in experience and level from 1800+ players to beginners. Don't have a paddle or ping pong balls? Don't sweat it! Another member would be happy to lend you their second paddle until you feel comfortable enough to buy your own. Don't let that stop you from coming out!

   Club Equipment


We have four beautiful tables and members who are always ready to play!

   Meeting Location


We meet three times a week at Maggie Trejo Supercenter (see Meeting Info above for days and times). Maggie Trejo is a city-owned community recreation center. They provide us with a large, high-ceilinged, gymnasium-like space to setup our tables.
